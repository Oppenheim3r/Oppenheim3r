Welcome! I'm Husam Gameel, known in the cybersecurity community as Oppenheim3r. I'm a passionate cybersecurity professional with extensive experience in penetration testing, red team operations, and defensive security strategies.

My journey in cybersecurity spans across multiple domains, from advanced exploit development and buffer overflow techniques to implementing robust SIEM rules and threat hunting methodologies. I specialize in bridging the gap between offensive and defensive security through comprehensive adversary emulation and purple team exercises.

Through this blog, I share my research, findings, and practical insights gained from real-world security assessments and continuous learning. My goal is to contribute to the cybersecurity community by providing detailed technical content that helps both aspiring and experienced security professionals enhance their skills.
